We believe in the quality of our furniture and want you to feel confident with your purchase. That's why we offer a 12-month warranty on all our furniture products. The warranty does not cover normal wear and tear, damages caused by misuse or neglect, or issues arising from improper assembly or care. For detailed information on what's covered and excluded, please refer to the warranty card included with your furniture purchase.
